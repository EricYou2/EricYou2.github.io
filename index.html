About Me



Hi! My name is Eric and am currently a sophomore studying Computer Science + Math at Washington University in St. Louis. I have always taken an interest in problem-solving as I enjoy passing the time doing crosswords, solving rubik's cubes, and coding.



I am able to work with a variety of languages, but the ones I am most comfortable with are Python and Java. Looking through my projects, I have many experiences tackling front end developments but still have a solid understanding of back end capabilities through SQL.



Throughout my college experience so far, the relevant coursework I have taken covers important topics like data structures and algorithms, mobile application development, and rapid prototyping and deployment. Outside the classroom, I am a Teaching Assistant for the data structures curriculum and a co-lead for the full stack special interest group for ACM at WashU.
